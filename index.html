About us


SSH Properties is an emerging professional, full service real estate consulting agency offering comprehensive range of services covering all types of properties including - Residential, Offices, Industrial, land, Warehouse and Retail Spaces throughout Chennai and rest of Tamilnadu, India. We are a niche player catering to the real estate needs of High Net worth Individual (HNI) and Non-Resident Indian (NRI) clients.


Our Client Centric approach focus on key mantras – Best in Class Service, Quality, Integrity, loyalty, and professionalism. We have highly motivated, knowledgeable and qualified professionals, will understand and cater Customer real estate needs by identifying the properties, Schedule Site visit, negotiate rate and get the best rate for the Customer, Close the deal on time with Customer Satisfaction. We build a strong long-standing relationship with Customer and they are always happy to refer us to their families, Friends and Collogues.
